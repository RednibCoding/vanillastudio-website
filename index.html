Powerful Tools

Everything You Need

Browse, customize, animate, and export — all from one app.

Browse MPQ Archives

Explore the full WoW 1.12.1 data files. Browse creatures, items, world objects, and character models through an intuitive file tree and searchable NPC/item lists.

Full Character System

Equip armor, weapons, helmets, and capes on any character model. Change skin color, face, hairstyle, and facial hair. Save and load character configurations.

Animation Playback

Play any animation on any model — walk, run, attack, cast, dance, and hundreds more. Control playback speed and scrub through frames.

Sprite Strip Export

Render animations as transparent PNG sprite strips — perfect for 2D games, pixel art pipelines, or sprite-based engines. Choose from 8-way isometric camera presets.

Multi-Format Export

Export to OBJ with materials and textures, glTF 2.0 with full skeleton and skinning, or IMR binary format. Static, animated, and animation-only modes.

Camera Presets

8-way isometric, front, back, side, top, and three-quarter views. Deterministic camera positions for consistent sprite capture across all your models.
